About This Game

  • Princess Petorine takes back the castle.
  • The wizard Melona pursues her master.
  • The kunoichi Kuno chases after the stolen Book of Secrets.
  • Rosalie, the dark elf who protects the elf village.
Each of the four protagonists has a story to tell.


Strengthen your skills by defeating enemies and shopping in a shop!
Even if you don't have any money, you can still shop by part-time job.

Prorogue

Princess Petorine wakes up with a great tremor.
A curse overflows the sealed door and possesses the soldiers.
She escapes from the castle in the nick of time and meets companions who help her.
The adventure to reclaim the castle begins.
