Santander reports attributable profit of €5,241 million for the first half of 2023, increasing earnings per share by 13%

Revenues up 13% supported by solid income growth in all regions and global businesses. Revenue growth was particularly strong in Santander CIB (24%), Wealth Management & Insurance (25%) and PagoNxt (27%).

Euromoney names Santander “best bank in the world” for SMEs, financial inclusion and emerging markets

Santander has received three global awards for the first time at the 2023 Euromoney Awards for Excellence: ‘World’s Best Bank for SMEs’; ‘World’s Best Bank for Financial Inclusion’ and ‘World’s Best Bank for the Emerging Markets’.
